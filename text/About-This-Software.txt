# About This Software:
HEAT Labs is a community-driven companion app designed to support players of World of Tanks: HEAT. It provides in-depth data, analysis tools, and community resources to help players better understand game mechanics, improve performance, and stay informed. Built by players, for players, HEAT Labs acts as a central hub for statistics, knowledge sharing, and experimentation bringing clarity to complex systems while fostering a collaborative community.

# Features:
HEAT Labs offers a wide range of tools and resources aimed at enhancing player knowledge and engagement.

## Tank Statistics
Access detailed information on vehicles, including armour values, firepower characteristics, mobility metrics, and performance comparisons. Designed to help players evaluate strengths and weaknesses.

## Player Statistics
Track and analyze player performance over time. View historical data, trends, and key metrics to better understand playstyle and progression.

## Map Knowledge
Explore map layouts, strategic positions, terrain features, and tactical insights. Ideal for planning routes, understanding sightlines, and improving situational awareness.

## Community Guides
Browse guides created by experienced players covering tactics, mechanics, vehicle usage, and general gameplay tips written by the community, for the community.

## Playground
From useful tools to fun games, check out our playground. From tier list creators to soundboards and a game configuration tool, you'll likely find something here to keep you entertained.

## Game News
Stay informed with curated updates, patch highlights, and notable developments related to World of Tanks: HEAT.

## Asset Gallery
View and reference in-game assets such as Vehicles, Agents, Abilities, Backgrounds, Environments and many, many more. Chances are, if It's something in the game, you'll find it here.

## Tournaments
Follow both community and officially run tournaments and events or look up legacy tournaments and events for results, matches and winners.

## Discord Integration
Connect directly with the HEAT Labs community through our Discord integration, enabling discussions, feedback, and collaboration all in one handy, easy to access place.

HEAT Labs is continuously evolving, with features expanding based on community feedback and ongoing development. We hope to see you become part of the HEAT Labs community.

# Frequently Asked Questions:
Q: What is HEAT Labs?
A: HEAT Labs is a community-driven, open-source platform dedicated to providing comprehensive and accurate statistics for World of Tanks: HEAT players.

Q: Is HEAT Labs free to use?
A: Absolutely! HEAT Labs is 100% free, with no ads or premium tiers. It's built by the community, for the community.
